About Me

I spent my early childhood in a musical family, where I learned a few instruments, and started drawing, When I was 12, I was drawing my own comic books, and discovered a website for kids made by MIT called Scratch. I originally used it to animate my comics, but inevitably moved on to coding my own games. Ever since then, I've been obsessed with video game design and developement. I made prototype after prototype, having family and friends playtest while I tweaked their design to get the reactions I wanted from people. I'm passionate about creating fun experiences that put smiles on peoples' faces, experiences that immerse people and expose them to new ideas, and uniquely new types of experiences that were previously never dreamt of.
